# SIGMA v1.8.0 — Classic Board Control Center + Laser Arcade Audio

## Purpose

Make the official Classic SIGMA Set feel like one premium brand package:

- Classic SIGMA Pieces
- Classic SIGMA Board
- Classic SIGMA Board audio/SFX profile

## Board UX/UI direction

The Classic SIGMA Board is redesigned as an integrated control-center board. The visual frame now includes raised metal slabs, command docks, energy rails, jewel corners, darker premium tiles, laser sweep accents, and owner orientation rails.

Design rule:

- Center grid stays clean and readable.
- Premium spectacle lives around the grid.
- Controls, timers, prompts, and reserve trays should feel like extensions of the board.
- Tabletop and non-tabletop layouts should eventually share the same board theme with different control ergonomics.

## Audio direction

SFX and music now lean toward laser-tag / upgrade / level-up energy while staying thoughtful and non-bassy.

- Menu music remains separate from board music.
- Board music remains board-based and adaptive.
- SFX are brighter, cleaner, and more arcade-premium.
- No SIGMA rules logic changed.

## Rules and Tutorial visuals

Rules snapshot cards, Tutorial, and in-game official visuals should use the Classic SIGMA Pieces and Classic SIGMA Board exclusively.
