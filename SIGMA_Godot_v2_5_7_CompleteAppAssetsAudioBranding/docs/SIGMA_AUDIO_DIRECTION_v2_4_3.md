# SIGMA Official Audio Direction v2.4.3

SIGMA audio should feel like premium 16-bit tactical arcade strategy: SNES/Sega Genesis inspired, glowing synths, FM bass, square wave leads, triangle arps, crystal chimes, clean digital drums, and focused board-game tension.

Avoid: cute retro platformer energy, vocals, crowd noise, guitar, orchestra, chaotic fighting-game clutter.

## Music states

- `MENU`: calm tactical arcade ambience, ~85 BPM.
- `GAMEPLAY_FOCUS`: thoughtful but energetic strategy loop, ~110 BPM.
- `BATTLE_TENSION`: BLITZ!, Peril, Overtime, final rounds, ~135 BPM.
- `PAUSE`: calm menu loop; gameplay music pauses underneath.
- `INTRO`: short 3-second logo identity sound.

## Core SFX events

- `button_tap`: tiny square-wave click/blip.
- `move`: crystal blip + soft synth click.
- `capture`: digital metallic impact + bass pulse.
- `deploy`: rising materialize sparkle.
- `illegal`: short low buzz.
- `peril`: low pulsing dark synth alert.
- `surrender` / `game_result`: premium 16-bit arcade stinger.
